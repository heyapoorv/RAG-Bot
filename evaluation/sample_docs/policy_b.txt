DOCUMENT: Amended Auto Insurance Policy — Version B (Effective: July 1, 2026)
Insurer: DocIntel Insurance Group | Policy Number: DAG-2026-B-001

Page 1: Core Coverage Terms (Amended)

The Amended Auto Insurance Policy (Version B) has a deductible of $750. (Increased from $500 in Version A)
The coverage limit for bodily injury is $150,000 per person and $500,000 per accident. (Increased from Version A)
The comprehensive coverage covers damage to your car caused by reasons other than collision, including fire, theft, windstorm, and hail. (Hail added in Version B)
The policy period runs from July 1, 2026 to June 30, 2027.
Payment must be received monthly by the 10th of each month. Late payments incur a $50 fee. (Due date and fee differ from Version A)
Uninsured motorist coverage is included up to $100,000 per person. (Doubled from Version A)
Medical payments coverage is $10,000 per person per accident. (Doubled from Version A)

Page 2: Additional Benefits (Amended)

The Roadside Assistance coverage provides 24/7 towing, flat tire change, battery jumpstart, lockout services, and fuel delivery. (Fuel delivery added in Version B)
There is a limit of 6 service calls per policy year. (Increased from 4 in Version A)
Rental car reimbursement is available up to $40 per day for a maximum of 14 days while your vehicle is being repaired due to a covered loss. (Increased rate and duration)
Any changes to the policy must be requested in writing and approved by the underwriters within 15 business days.
The cancellation notice period is 45 days. (Extended from 30 days in Version A)
Gap insurance is included as a standard feature for all financed or leased vehicles. (No longer optional — now standard)

Page 3: Claims & Dispute Resolution (Amended)

Claims must be filed within 90 days of the incident. (Extended from 60 days in Version A)
The insurer reserves the right to inspect the vehicle before approving a claim.
Disputes are resolved under the jurisdiction of the state of California. (Changed from Delaware in Version A)
Fraud or misrepresentation voids the policy immediately without refund.
New in Version B: A $250 claims processing fee applies to all claims under $1,000.
