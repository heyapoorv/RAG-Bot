DocIntel Insurance Policy Coverages.
Page 1:
The Standard Auto Insurance Policy has a deductible of $500.
The coverage limit for bodily injury is $100,000 per person and $300,000 per accident.
The comprehensive coverage covers damage to your car caused by reason other than collision, such as fire, theft, or windstorm.
The policy period runs from January 1, 2026 to December 31, 2026.
Payment must be received monthly by the 5th of each month. Late payments incur a $25 fee.

Page 2:
The Roadside Assistance coverage provides 24/7 towing, flat tire change, battery jumpstart, and lockout services.
There is a limit of 4 service calls per policy year.
Rental car reimbursement is available up to $30 per day for a maximum of 10 days while your vehicle is being repaired due to a covered loss.
Any changes to the policy must be requested in writing and approved by the underwriters.
