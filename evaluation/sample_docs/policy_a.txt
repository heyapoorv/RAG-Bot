DOCUMENT: Standard Auto Insurance Policy — Version A (Effective: January 1, 2026)
Insurer: DocIntel Insurance Group | Policy Number: DAG-2026-A-001

Page 1: Core Coverage Terms

The Standard Auto Insurance Policy (Version A) has a deductible of $500.
The coverage limit for bodily injury is $100,000 per person and $300,000 per accident.
The comprehensive coverage covers damage to your car caused by reasons other than collision, such as fire, theft, or windstorm.
The policy period runs from January 1, 2026 to December 31, 2026.
Payment must be received monthly by the 5th of each month. Late payments incur a $25 fee.
Uninsured motorist coverage is included up to $50,000 per person.
Medical payments coverage is $5,000 per person per accident.

Page 2: Additional Benefits

The Roadside Assistance coverage provides 24/7 towing, flat tire change, battery jumpstart, and lockout services.
There is a limit of 4 service calls per policy year.
Rental car reimbursement is available up to $30 per day for a maximum of 10 days while your vehicle is being repaired due to a covered loss.
Any changes to the policy must be requested in writing and approved by the underwriters.
The cancellation notice period is 30 days.
Gap insurance is available as an optional add-on for financed or leased vehicles.

Page 3: Claims & Dispute Resolution

Claims must be filed within 60 days of the incident.
The insurer reserves the right to inspect the vehicle before approving a claim.
Disputes are resolved under the jurisdiction of the state of Delaware.
Fraud or misrepresentation voids the policy immediately without refund.
